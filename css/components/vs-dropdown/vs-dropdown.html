  • Apply placement modifiers (`vs-dropdown--up`, `--down`, `--start`, `--end`) and alignment modifiers (`vs-dropdown--align-start`, `--align-end`) together to control menu anchoring.
  • Behavior modifiers: `vs-dropdown--auto-close` (default) closes on selection/outside; `vs-dropdown--no-auto-close` keeps menu open after selecting; `vs-dropdown--manual` disables outside-click and Escape closing.
  • Surface modifiers layer on top of default styling: use `--dark` for inverse menus, `--bordered` for outlines, `--rounded` or `--square` to override radius.
  • Use `vs-dropdown--scrollable` when menus exceed the recommended max height; combine with size modifiers (`--sm`, `--md`, `--lg`) to adjust trigger and menu density.
