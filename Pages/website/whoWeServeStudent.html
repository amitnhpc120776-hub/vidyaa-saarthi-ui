Mastery-Based Evaluation

Every topic in the chapter is evaluated using a clear, three-level mastery model.

  • Recall — Ability to remember facts, definitions, formulas, and direct information related to a topic.
  • Understanding — Ability to explain the concept with conceptual clarity rather than memorization.
  • Application — Ability to apply, justify, or logically reason using the concept in more complex or unfamiliar situations.
