Anyone above 18 years of age can use Vidyaasaarthi independently. Users below 18 may access the platform with parental or guardian consent. We welcome students, teachers, and educators.

Courses & Pricing

Vidyaasaarthi offers both free and paid courses. Our Free plan provides access to basic topics and limited explanations. Paid plans (Basic, Pro, and Enterprise) unlock unlimited explanations, personalized learning paths, and advanced features. Check our Pricing page for more details.

Enrollment is simple! Log in to your Vidyaasaarthi account, browse available courses, and select the one you want. If it's a paid course, follow the payment process. You'll get instant access once your payment is confirmed.

We accept multiple payment methods for your convenience, including:
  • Credit and Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.)
  • Net Banking

Refunds & Support

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied within 7 days of purchase, contact us at support@vidyaasarthi.com with your order details, and we'll process a full refund within 5-7 business days.

You can cancel your subscription anytime from your account settings. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing cycle. No refunds are provided for partial months unless within the 7-day money-back period.
